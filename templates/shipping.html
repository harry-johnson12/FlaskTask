Shipping & delivery

Fast fulfillment with transparent tracking.

Gearloom ships worldwide from three regional hubs. Below you’ll find the service levels and cut-off times we operate to keep builds on schedule.

Dispatch speed

  • Orders placed before 18:00 local hub time ship same-day.
  • Weekend orders leave Monday morning.
  • Expedited handling for lab-critical replacements — email logistics@gearloom.io.

Service tiers

Express

1–2 days within North America & EU, 3 days APAC.

Standard

3–5 days tracked delivery with carbon-offset carriers.

Freight

Bulk crates palletized with environmental monitoring.

Customs & paperwork

International orders include commercial invoices, compliance declarations, and HS codes. Share your importer reference or project code during checkout to have it applied automatically — the upcoming AI planner will populate this data for you.
