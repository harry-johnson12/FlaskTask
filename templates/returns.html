Returns & exchanges

Confidence for prototyping and production.

We stand behind every part we stock. If something isn’t right, we’ll repair, replace, or refund it quickly so your roadmap stays on track.

30-day return window

Unused items in resellable condition can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Contact support@gearloom.io with your order ID to generate a prepaid label.

Hardware assurance

If a component fails in normal use within 12 months, our lab team will inspect and overnight a replacement. Include logs or telemetry if available — it feeds the validation graph that powers future AI recommendations.

Non-returnable items

  • Custom-cut cabling and fabricated panels
  • Software licenses and activation keys
  • Consumables opened from sealed packaging (thermal paste, adhesives)

If you’re unsure whether an item qualifies, reach out before unsealing — we’ll clarify options and log guidance for the AI assistant.
